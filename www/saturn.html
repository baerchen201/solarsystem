Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is well-known for its stunning system of rings. It is a gas giant with a diameter of 116,460 kilometers, making it the second-largest planet in the solar system. The rings are made up of ice, rock, and dust particles, and Saturn's atmosphere is primarily composed of hydrogen and helium.

Size: 116,460 km in diameter

Distance from Sun: 1.43 billion kilometers
