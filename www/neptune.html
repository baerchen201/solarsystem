Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in our solar system. It is a gas giant known for its deep blue color, caused by methane in its atmosphere. It has a diameter of approximately 49,244 kilometers, making it the fourth largest planet by diameter. Neptune is famous for its strong winds, which are the fastest recorded in the solar system, reaching speeds of up to 2,100 kilometers per hour.

Size: 49,244 km in diameter

Distance from Sun: 4.5 billion kilometers
