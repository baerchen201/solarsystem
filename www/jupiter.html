Jupiter

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest planet in our solar system. With a diameter of 139,820 kilometers, it is a gas giant composed mostly of hydrogen and helium. Jupiter has a famous storm called the Great Red Spot, which has been raging for over 400 years. Jupiter has 79 known moons, including the four largest moons, the Galilean moons: Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.

Size: 139,820 km in diameter

Distance from Sun: 778 million kilometers
