Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life. It has a diverse environment with water bodies, an atmosphere containing oxygen, and a variety of ecosystems.

Size: Diameter: 12,742 kilometers (7,918 miles). Mass: 1 Earth mass.

Distance from the Sun: 149.6 million kilometers (93 million miles).

Atmosphere: Earth's atmosphere is composed mainly of nitrogen (78%) and oxygen (21%), with traces of argon, carbon dioxide, and other gases.

Interesting Fact: Earth is the only planet known to have liquid water on its surface, which is essential for life as we know it.
