Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun. It has a very thin atmosphere, so it cannot retain heat. The surface temperature varies greatly, reaching up to 430°C during the day and dropping to -180°C at night.

Size: Diameter: 4,880 kilometers (3,032 miles). Mass: 0.055 Earth masses.

Distance from the Sun: 57.9 million kilometers (36 million miles).

Atmosphere: Mercury has a very thin atmosphere composed mainly of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, helium, and potassium.

Interesting Fact: Despite being closest to the Sun, Mercury is not the hottest planet. Venus holds that title due to its thick atmosphere.
