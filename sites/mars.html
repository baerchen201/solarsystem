Mars

Mars, known as the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance, is the fourth planet from the Sun. It has a thin atmosphere and is the most explored planet other than Earth.

Size: Diameter: 6,779 kilometers (4,212 miles). Mass: 0.107 Earth masses.

Distance from the Sun: 227.9 million kilometers (141.6 million miles).

Atmosphere: Mars has a very thin atmosphere, mainly composed of carbon dioxide (95%) with traces of nitrogen and argon.

Interesting Fact: Mars has the largest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons, which is about 22 kilometers (13.6 miles) high.
